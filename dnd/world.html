Major Celestial Bodies

    the motions of mortals are dictated more than they know by the motions of the stars

    The celestial bodies most important in the eyes of mortals. These etchings on the fabric of reality harbour the mortal life and magic of the universe.

    Surna

    A massive flaming ball, bringing light to the land.

    Doran

    A small silver rock, orbiting exactly opposite Surna.

    Ranmar

    An enigmatic celestial, completely enveloping Surna every two-hundred years, plunging the world into darkness.

    Myr

    The disc where mortals walk.
