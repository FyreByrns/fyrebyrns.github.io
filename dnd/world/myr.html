Myr

    The disc where mortals walk.

    All life sits atop a massive disc, ten thousand across, and unknowably deep. The few who claim to have reached the edge recount torrents of water beyond your wildest imagination vanishing over the edge.

    Dwarves
    pc known limited immortality runic scribes completely mundane dornal mortal ranmic mortal
    A hardy race with a limited claim to immortality.
    Dwarves are immortal unless by outside influence.
    When in regular contact with the light of either moon, Dwarves are mortal.
    Dwarves have no grasp on the arcane, and are completely incapable of casting magic themselves. Dwarves can, however, etch power into runes. While every dwarf can etch limited power into basic runes, great power and complex runes require years of experience.
    Dwarves stand 3'6" to 5' tall.
    Dwarves are stocky, often nearly as wide as they are tall, solid with muscle.
    Their arms are often nearly as thick as their thighs. Some grow rotund with age, though just as strong.

    Dwarves wear their black or red hair in thick braids.
    Dwarves sport any natural colour of skin.

    Elves
    pc known limited immortality arcane forgotten
    A secretive race with limited claim to immortality.
    Elves are immortal unless by outside influence.
    Elves have long passed from the memory of other races, only living on in myth and legend.
    When an elf does appear in civilization, most react with fear and distrust.
    Elves are tall and willowy, and generally dislike frivolous matters.
    Elves never sleep, and will age twice as fast as humans until their mid twenties, at which point they will barely age at all for centuries.

    Elves have fair hair, ranging from bright white with a green tint to dirty blonde. Very rarely, an elf will have striking red hair.
    Elves sport any natural colour of skin.

    Gnomes
    pc known dornal fleeting
    A friendly race with an affinity for craft.
    Gnomes are quick to trust and slow to anger, but will never forget a slight.
    Gnomes are said to be born from Doran, and under the moonlight they are refreshed and strengthened.
    You can often find Gnomes on a clear night lying out in a field, or frolicking about the meadows.
    Gnomes are short and slight, standing on average a good foot shorter than Dwarves.

    All Gnomes have some tone of brown hair.
    Gnomes sport any natural colour of skin.

    Living Stone
    pc limited immortality forgotten
    Sentient statues adorned with runes of power.
    Created in an era forgotten even to the Elves, the Living Stone walk Myr for reasons unknown to even them.

    Largely forgotten in the current age, Living Stone are hidden across the world, hidden by ages of dust blowing over them.
    The look of a Living Stone will tell a story about their existance.
    A Stone which came to a gentle existance will have defined corners, perhaps delicate filigree adorning every inch of their body.
    One which has lived through harsh conditions will be eroded smooth.
    Whenever a Living Stone is thinking, countless runes sparkle like the stars all over their bodies.
